Artichoke Chicken

Description

Artichoke chicken is a delightful meal featuring a lot of flavor and a great amount of protein. The dinner can be paired with a pasta and veggie, or will be complete by itself. This meal will certainly make the consumer satiated.

Ingredients

  • olive oil
  • 1 can artichoke hearts
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease baking dish with the olive oil.
  2. Mix together artichoke hearts, parmesan cheese, and mayonnaise.
  3. Add chicken to baking dish, and add mixture on the top of the chicken.
  4. Bake uncovered for 30 minutes.
