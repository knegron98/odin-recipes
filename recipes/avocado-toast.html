Avocado Toast

Description

Avocado toast is a quick and easy snack, breakfast, or lunch. This meal includes a healthy source of fats and a yummy combination of flavors. This is one of my favorite recipes.

Ingredients

  • avocado
  • honey
  • bread
  • red pepper flakes
  • salt

Steps

  1. Toast your bread of choice.
  2. Mash the avocado and spread it onto the toast.
  3. Drizzle honey over the top.
  4. Sprinkle red pepper flakes and salt onto the top.
  5. If you want, you can add boiled egg slices or a fried egg for additional protein.
